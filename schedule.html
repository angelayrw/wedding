Schedule of the Day

13:30 - Welcome

Guests are invited to gather at Walnut Tree Court prior to the ceremony. Please enter the college through the Porter’s Lodge on Silver Street, where directions will be provided.

14:30 - Ceremony

The wedding ceremony will take place in the chapel. Confetti will be provided for a celebratory moment following the ceremony.

15:30 - Cocktail Reception

Join us for a cocktail reception with canapés in the Old Court. This will be an opportunity for guests to take official photographs with the couple.

17:30 - Dinner

A three-course dinner will be served in the Old Hall. Details of the menu can be found on the menu page.

20:00 - Evening Reception

The celebration continues with our evening reception. Get ready to enjoy the music as a Jazz band awaits you in the Old Kitchen.
